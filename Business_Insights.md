# SQL Insights

The SQL analysis revealed several important patterns in user behavior and revenue performance.

1. **The dataset contains 21,409 user interaction events generated by 10,000 unique users, providing a comprehensive view of customer activity.**

2. **User engagement decreases at every stage of the funnel, with the largest drop-off occurring between the Checkout and Purchase stages.**

3. **Google Ads generated the highest revenue among all marketing channels, making it the strongest acquisition source.**

4. **The South region contributed the highest overall revenue, indicating stronger customer engagement and purchasing behavior compared to other regions.**

5. **Revenue distribution across devices is relatively balanced, suggesting users actively interact with the platform regardless of their preferred device.**

# Dashboard Insights

The interactive Power BI dashboard highlights key business metrics and customer behavior through visual analysis.

1. **Only 1,004 out of 3,456 users who reached Checkout completed a purchase, indicating a significant loss of potential customers.**

2. **Google Ads consistently outperformed Email, Social Media, and Organic channels in revenue generation.**

3. **The South region recorded the highest revenue, while the remaining regions showed relatively similar performance.**

4. **The customer funnel clearly shows a continuous decline in users from Browse to Purchase, emphasizing opportunities to improve conversion rates.**

5. **The dashboard combines funnel, revenue, and device analytics in a single view, enabling quick identification of customer behavior patterns and business performance.**

# Business Recommendations

Based on the analysis, the following recommendations can help improve business performance:

### 1. Optimize the Checkout Experience
The largest customer drop-off occurs between Checkout and Purchase. Simplifying the checkout process, reducing unnecessary steps, and improving payment reliability can increase conversions.

### 2. Increase Investment in High-Performing Channels
Google Ads generated the highest revenue. Allocating a larger portion of the marketing budget to this channel while optimizing underperforming channels can improve overall return on investment.

### 3. Replicate Success Across Regions
The South region demonstrated the strongest revenue performance. Analyzing successful marketing strategies and customer behavior in this region can help improve performance in other regions.
